Our Products

What you pay your staff is an ever-growing expense, and it's likely to be your biggest one. If not carefully monitored and analysed, it will undoubtedly contribute to declining profits.
However, it's rare that a business has the time or resources available to explore this important issue thoroughly.

Staff Tracker Lite

Our flagship product, this is the best introduction to our Staff Tracker. Although named ‘Lite’, it serves some of our most demanding customers with ease. This option offers minimal set up investment, and gives you all of the reporting options of the full version.

Staff Tracker Classic

Our full version of the Staff Tracker is for the experienced user who wants full control of the system. This product comes with advanced customisation options not available in the Lite version.

Staff Tracker Managed

For our customers that don’t wish to operate the Staff Tracker but still needs its reporting and alerts, we offer a fully managed service. Our expert team will manage the account completely.

Outstanding Features

Successfully used across the world for almost two decades, Telelog Staff Tracker was created by executives, for executives, and continually undergoes improvements to make it an essential tool in today's market. Our feature set includes:

Geo Location

You can see on a map from where your staff are clocking in and out at all times.

Advanced Biometrics

Our Mobile Applications all feature multi-user biometric facial recognition.

Live Recording

Our system record live image and sound recording of log entries as they happen.

Payroll Time Calculations

Exact and approved time calculations that export directly to Sage, Intuit, etc.

Cloud Based

Nothing to install. No software upgrades, no maintenance fees. No hardware costs

Extended Reporting

Pre made as well as custom reporting and graphing to cover any circumstances.

Fully Scalable

It does not matter if you have 10 or 25000 staff. The system scales with you.

Choice of Input Methods

Supported are Mobile App, desk phones, fingerprint & palm readers, QR Code, NFC or OTP.
